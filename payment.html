Payment Options

We support a comprehensive set of payment options tailored for factories, warehouses, and supplier networks. Choose the mix that best fits your procurement and receivable workflows.

Card Payments (PCI-DSS compliant)

Domestic and international cards via tokenized credentials. Supports 3D Secure and fraud prevention tools.

  • Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover
  • Digital wallets: Apple Pay, Google Pay, Samsung Pay
  • Tokenization and secure vaults

ACH & Bank Transfers

Electronic payments directly from bank accounts with automated reconciliation feeds.

  • ACH (US), Bacs (UK), SEPA (EU)
  • Real-time or batched settlements
  • Automated remittance advice and remittance matching

Invoice & Payables Automation

Self-serve supplier payments, batch processing, and portal-based approvals.

  • Net terms customization (e.g., Net 15, Net 30)
  • Early payment discounts and dynamic discounting
  • Supplier portals with status tracking

Mobile Wallet & QR Payments

Fast checkout at plants or distribution centers using mobile wallets and QR scans.

  • QR invoice payments, in-person mobile POS
  • Offline-safe prompts with online reconciliation

Pay-by-Parts / Fleet Billing

Split payments for mixed procurement orders or fleet-related charges.

  • Partial payments, installments, or milestone-based billing
  • Per-tenant or per-site billing for multi-facility operations

Vendor / Voucher Payments

Prepaid vouchers and vendor-specific payment methods for supplier onboarding.

  • Voucher issuance and redemption tracking
  • ERP integration for voucher issuance
